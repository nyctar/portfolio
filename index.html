About Me

“What story do you want to tell?”

Every project I make, I ask myself this question first. As a creative technologist passionate for data science and visualization, I always try to be intentional — to see the lived experiences behind the numbers.

I’ve dabbled in different areas of technology: Data Science, XR Development, Interaction Design, and others. But, recently, I’ve been specializing on Data Visualization. I’m interested in understanding how to best represent the nature of data through visual encoding.

Currently, I work as a Teaching Assistant at Extern, supporting tech-related externships that make college students ready for the industry.
