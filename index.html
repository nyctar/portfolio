About Me

Every project I make, I ask myself this question first.

“What story do you want to tell?”

As a creative technologist specializing on Data Science & Visualization, I’m interested in understanding how to best represent the nature of data through visual encoding with intention — to see the lived experiences behind the numbers.

I’ve also gained transferrable skills by dabbling in different areas of technology, XR Development, Game Development, Interaction Design, and others. Currently, I work as a Teaching Assistant at Extern, supporting tech-related externships that aim to make college students ready for the industry.

If you're interested in working with me, hit me up!
