Class Project – 2025

Historical Document Visualization

As a project submission for my Introduction to Infographics and Data Visualization class, I made a historical timeline of Benajah Jay Antrim’s journey across Mexico documented in his journals. The timeline was made using ArcGIS story maps and sentiment analysis of the journal transcript to give more context of his travels.

Class Project – 2025

South China Sea Incident Visualization

As a project submission for my Introduction to Infographics and Data Visualization class, I made graphics in Adobe Illustrator by analyzing data from the South China Sea Data Initiative with ArcGIS and R. I identified the density and clustering of China-related hostile incidents in the area compared to China’s nine-dash line claim and each country's exclusive economic zone.

Class Project – 2025

3D Music Visualization

As a project submission for my Front-end Fundamentals class, I made a music visualizer that uses the Fast Fourier transform function in p5.js to get the amplitude and frequency of a sound. The change in these values are visualized by parameter mapping it to the scale, color, and number of spheres in a 3D grid.

Snap Inc. – 2024

Views of Stonewall

During my apprenticeship program at Snap Inc., I collaborated with a team of 3 to build a Custom Location AR lens focused on the heritage of the Stonewall Inn and the 1969 Stonewall Riots. Using AR as a new medium of protest, we aim to uplift transgender women of color who face marginalization and erasure to this day. The AR lens was selected as a finalist for the Best Artistic Lens category of Snap AR’s 2024 Lens Fest Awards.

Class Project – 2024

Building Scene Level Image Classification

As a project submission for my Introduction to Machine Learning class, I used CNNs to categorize building scene level images, achieving 86% test accuracy with a traditional CNN model and 93% with a transfer learning model after fine-tuning. The process included data augmentation, class weights, and callbacks for the model with GradCAM for result interpretability.
